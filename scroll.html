1996

The year is 1996 and a deadly virus has been released and wiping out most of humanity, the survivors forced to live underground. A group known only as the Army of the Twelve Monkeys is believed to be responsible for releasing the virus. Flash forward to 2035, James Cole (Bruce Willis) is a prisoner beneath the city of Philadelphia and has been chosen to be trained and sent back in time to help develop a cure for the virus. During his lengthy and rigorous training in a post-apocalyptic Philadelphia, James set foot above ground for the first time in decades, witnessing the true terror of the outfall of the 12 monkeys virus.

2035

During his period of training, Cole starts to have recurring nightmares about a shooting and a chase scene in an airport involving him. As he finds himself alone, taking samples and getting acclimated, he encounters a bear that frightens him and induces the same flashback as his dreams. The scientists in charge of his training never communicate with him in person and retain a mask of anonymity, adding to his growing discomfort and slipping grip on his sanity.
